Panarottis

Panarottis is a South African restaurant chain founded in 1990, specializing in Italian cuisine, particularly pizza and pasta. With a family-friendly atmosphere, it offers a diverse menu featuring wood-fired pizzas, classic pasta dishes, salads, and desserts. Known for its affordability and fresh ingredients, Panarottis caters to all ages and dietary preferences. The chain also provides franchise opportunities for entrepreneurs interested in joining the restaurant industry, offering training and support to franchisees. Overall, Panarottis is renowned for its delicious Italian fare and welcoming environment.
