Checkers-Hyper

Checkers Hyper is a prominent South African supermarket chain owned by the Shoprite Group. Established in 1956, it offers a wide variety of products including groceries, household items, electronics, and clothing. Known for its large format stores, Checkers Hyper provides quality products at competitive prices and frequently runs promotions and specials for customers. The chain also engages in community initiatives and charitable projects.
